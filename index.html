Precision Power for Critical Applications

At Vaishno Devi Traders, we specialize in servo-controlled voltage stabilizers, pure sine wave inverters, and high-efficiency UPS systems designed for precise voltage regulation and dependable backup power.

Established in 1997, our in-house manufacturing and rigorous testing ensure high performance, low losses, and long operational life. Our systems safeguard sensitive electronics, minimize downtime, and improve overall power quality across industrial and residential installations.

We are also authorized dealers of Amptek Batteries, offering high-capacity, long-life energy storage for reliable backup.
